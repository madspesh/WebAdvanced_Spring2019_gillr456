Is it accessible?

For those with low vision, it can be difficult to read text with a colour contrast ratio lower than 3.0. As a designer, your work could actually be illegible for many users.

Are you aware of which colour combinations are accessible and which are not? Take this simple quiz and test your designers eye.

To learn more about web accessibility, consult the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) official guidelines or check out the WebAIM website.
